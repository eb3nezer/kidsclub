Use this page to upload a CSV file containing student information. The following columns are imported:

  • name - The student's name
  • given name - The student's first given name
  • family name - The student's family name
  • phone - A contact phone number
  • email - A contact email address
  • school - The name of the student's school
  • age - The student's age in years
  • school year - The year the student is in at school
  • gender - The student's gender. (This column can also be called 'sex')
  • emergency contact - An emergency contact name for this student. (This column can also be called 'contact name' or 'parent')
  • contact relationship - The emergency contact's relationship to the child, such as "parent". (This column can also be called 'relationship')
  • special instructions - Any special instructions, like allergies or food intolerances.
  • team - The name of the team to assign this child to. Note that this must be an exact match to an existing team name.

The only required field is either "name" or both "given name" and "family name".
